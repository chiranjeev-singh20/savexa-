FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Savexa a replacement for emergency services?

No. Savexa helps you act quickly while you contact or wait for professional help. For any life-threatening situation, call your local emergency number immediately.

Where do my camera frames go?

Frames are processed in-memory only. Nothing is stored on our servers and nothing is shared with third parties.

Does it work offline?

After the first load, the guidance library is cached and works offline. Live scans require connectivity for the vision model.

Which injuries are supported?

Burns, lacerations, abrasions, sprains, nosebleeds, and more — with new categories added each release.
